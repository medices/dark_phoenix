Renewable Energy and Design

Renewable Energy and Design

Two modest solar electric systems provide for all of our electricity needs in our home, the farm, and apprentice housing. We also have a van with a mobile solar electric system, and our farmstand refrigeration is solar powered. Our photovoltaic systems have been installed with the support of Bob Higgins, Atlantic Alternative Power in Gill, MA. Homepower Magazine (homepower.com) is an invaluable source of information on all aspects of renewable energy.

Energy Efficient Design and Vehicle Power

We’ve designed and built all of the structures on the Seeds of Solidarity land by combining conventional building techniques with materials that are recycled or energy efficient. We use biodiesel, a vegetable-based fuel in our family car, farm truck, and the SOL Patrol E350 van. We blend biodiesel with fossil fuel diesel, depending on vehicle fuel system sensitivity. Imagine the 440,000 school buses across the nation running on biodiesel, reducing childhood asthma, exposure to carcinogens, and reliance on imported oil.
